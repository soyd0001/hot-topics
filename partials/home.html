The Value of Money

The value of money is determined by the demand for it. And demand changes over time. Even though it seems easy to understand when it is considered the time is the only factor that affects the value of money, in reality there are lots of factors that are play complex and indirect roles. Value of a currency depends on the production of that country, economic performance and also political relations with other countries. A currency might increase its numeric vaue while loosing its purchasing power.Time not only affects the value of money during a continuous period, it also has power to determine the current and future value of it. Is it better to have 10.000 US Dollars now that having 12.000 Us Dollars in two years? To answer this question a complex formula need to be run to calculate the expected value of future money with current demand and expected trend over time.

GMO Food

GMO foods which also known as genetically engineered foods are foods created in a labratory by making changes on their DNA by genetical engineers. As all foods on the market, GMO foods also requires some safety assessments before introduced to the markets. Currently there is not enough evidence to say that GMO foods are dangerous and not good for human health. Test that have been made on humans who consumed GMO foods and compared with a control group. the control group haven't consumed any GMO foods. Results show that there is no difference between those groups. Still, more time and tests are needed to be fully sure and say that GMO foods has no side effects on human health.
